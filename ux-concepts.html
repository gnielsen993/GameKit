Long-press to flag

No mode to manage. The most natural mapping: tap the cell you want to reveal, hold the cell you're uncertain about.

✓ Mirrors native iOS patterns (long-press in Files, Photos)
✓ Frees layout space — no pill needed

✗ Less discoverable than a visible toggle
✗ Flagging requires hold patience

Nonogram

Tap = fill cell black. Long-press = mark cell with ×. No mode pill needed.

Filling (primary action) is always just a tap. Marking (secondary) requires intent — which is appropriate since marks are just helpers.
