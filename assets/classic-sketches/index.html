GameKit — Classic preset sketches

Restomod arcade direction: old-school design language, modern execution. Three variants × three screens (Home, Minesweeper, Solitaire) so we can pick the right Classic baseline before refactoring DesignKit. Each variant is a candidate "feel"; per-game contexts then layer their own tweaks (Mines = Win 95 greys, Sudoku = green felt, etc) on top of the chosen foundation.

A — Coin-Op

Saturated jewel-tone bg, neon-yellow accent with subtle glow, condensed display type. Pinball / Galaga marquee energy. Loudest of the three — "you stepped into the arcade."
