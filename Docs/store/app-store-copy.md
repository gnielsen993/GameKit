### Body

GameDrawer 1.4 adds two word games, bringing the drawer to eight. Both support Video Mode, resume-where-you-left-off, and stats, day one.

**Five Letter.** Guess a five-letter word in six tries. Every guess colors the tiles — green for a correct letter in place, yellow for one that belongs elsewhere — and the on-screen keyboard tracks what you've ruled out. There's a new Daily word for everyone, plus Unlimited rounds whenever you want more. Guesses are validated against a 15,000-word list, so real words always count, and answers come from a curated pool of 1,000 common words.

**Word Grid.** Trace a path through adjacent letters on a 4×4 board to build words — the longer the word, the more it scores. Swipe across tiles to spell. Play Timed against the clock or Relaxed with no pressure, and found words stay listed as you go (on a side rail on wider screens). Every board is generated to guarantee a fair set of words to find, so you're never staring at a dead grid.

The feedback is intentional: complete a row, column, or 3×3 box and those cells briefly glow. Place the last of a number and the pad button spring-bounces before settling into a diagonal strike-through. It knows what you just did.

**Still no ads. Still no coins. Still no accounts.** That's not a marketing line — it's a design constraint. GameDrawer has no ad SDK, no coin economy, no energy system, and no paywalled features. You download it once.
