    # GameKit Privacy Policy

    **Last updated:** 2026-04-27
    **Effective:** at App Store launch

    ## TL;DR

    GameKit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on servers we control. The app works fully offline. Optional iCloud sync uses your private iCloud database — Apple-encrypted, accessible only to you.

    ## Data we collect

    **None.** This is the App Store Connect privacy nutrition label answer for GameKit:

    > **Data Not Collected**

    The reasoning, in plain language:

    > CloudKit private DB, encrypted, dev has no access; no analytics SDKs; MetricKit not integrated → Data Not Collected

    ## What that means in detail

    ### 1. CloudKit private database (optional, off by default)

    If you choose to sign in with Apple from Settings → SYNC, your gameplay statistics (game count, best times, win/loss outcomes) are stored in **your private iCloud database**, encrypted by Apple, accessible only to you.

    - We (the developer) **cannot** read your data. Apple's CloudKit private-database design does not grant developers access to user records.
    - Your data syncs to other devices signed in to the same iCloud account.
    - You can stop sync at any time by signing out (Settings → Apple ID → Sign in with Apple → GameKit → Stop using Apple ID).
    - Sign-in is **always optional**. The full game works without signing in.

    ### 2. No analytics, no tracking

    GameKit ships with **zero** analytics SDKs:

    - No Google Analytics. No Firebase. No Mixpanel. No Sentry. No Bugsnag. No third-party crash reporters.
    - No `MetricKit` integration in v1.0. Apple's Xcode Organizer is the only crash-data source available to the developer, and it surfaces aggregated, anonymized data only.
    - No advertising IDs. No fingerprinting. No location.

    ### 3. No accounts (other than optional iCloud)

    GameKit does not require you to create an account. The only sign-in surface is Sign in with Apple, used purely to gate iCloud sync — and even that is optional.

    ### 4. No ads, no in-app purchases, no fake currencies

    GameKit has no ads, no banner ads, no interstitials, no rewarded videos. No coins. No energy systems. No subscriptions. No tracking pixels.

    ## Your rights

    Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on your behalf. To remove all GameKit data:

    - Delete the app — local data is removed.
    - If you signed in to iCloud sync: System Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage → GameKit → Delete from iCloud.

    ## Children's privacy

    GameKit does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for all ages.

    ## Changes to this policy

    If we ever change what GameKit collects (we don't plan to), the App Store nutrition label and this policy will both be updated before the change ships in a binary.

    ## Contact

    Questions? Email: support@lauterstar.com

    ---

    *This policy applies to GameKit, published by Lauterstar. The companion design system, [DesignKit](https://github.com/<user>/DesignKit), follows the same posture.*
