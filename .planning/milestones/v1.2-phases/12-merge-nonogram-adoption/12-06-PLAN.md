    Per CONTEXT D-12-MATRIX, the matrix has **24 rows = 2 games × 2 representative difficulties × 6 PiP zones**. The "representative difficulties" pick:

    - **Merge:** Two game modes — `winMode` (the 2048 target) and `infinite` (free play past 2048). Merge has no difficulty axis; modes are the closest analog.
    - **Nonogram:** **Tiny (5×5)** (easiest — verifies the small grid still feels comfortable) and **Medium (15×15 Hard)** (the squeeze case for hint legibility on Large zones; Plan 12-05's locked floor target).

    Middle Nonogram difficulties (`Small 10×10`, `Large 20×20`) inherit by geometric interpolation per the P11-VIDEO-MANUAL-CHECK precedent — verified only if the Tiny + Medium rows both pass.

    ## How to use

    For each row:

    1. Launch the app on iPhone 17 Pro Max simulator.
    2. Settings → Video Mode → On.
    3. Settings → Video location → select the row's `Zone`.
    4. Home → tap the row's game tile.
    5. For **Merge** rows: switch to the row's `Mode` via the compact-row's Restart-w-menu (slot 5 on Large zones) or nav-bar toolbar (Small zones). Run each gesture column:
       - **First-swipe** — tap-and-drag a tile direction; confirm the tiles merge or shift.
       - **Restart** — tap Restart (compact-row slot 5 / nav-bar icon); confirm a fresh board.
       - **Mode-change** — open the Restart Menu; pick the other mode; confirm the abandon-alert fires mid-game then board resets.
       - **Win/GameOver completes** — play to 2048 (Win) or board-locked (GameOver); confirm the end-state overlay appears.
    6. For **Nonogram** rows: switch to the row's `Difficulty` via the compact-row's Restart-w-menu (slot 5) or nav-bar toolbar. Run each gesture column:
       - **First-tap** — tap any cell; confirm fill (Place mode) or mark (Mark mode) appears.
       - **Slide-fill** — drag across multiple cells; confirm the smear lays down fills/marks along the path.
       - **Long-press mark** — long-press an unrevealed cell; confirm a mark appears (or fill, mode-dependent).
       - **Restart** — tap Restart; confirm a fresh puzzle appears.
       - **Win completes** — solve the puzzle; confirm confetti + end-state card.
    7. Mark Pass/Fail per row. Add notes for any deviation from baseline.

    For Nonogram Hard rows (esp. 22 + 23 — `.largeBottom` rows): additionally compare the rendered Hard board against the Plan 12-05 audit screenshots — final-render parity is the SC2 acceptance condition.
