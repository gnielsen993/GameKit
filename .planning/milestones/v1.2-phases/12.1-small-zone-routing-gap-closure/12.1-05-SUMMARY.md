## Lessons learned

- **Live-sim audit catches what plan-text and unit tests miss.** The original
  12.1-02/03/04 plan-checker passed 11/11 gates including byte-identity probes
  and acceptance criteria with exact grep strings, but the LAYOUT itself
  (stacked VStack interpretation of router intent) was wrong. No automated
  check would have caught it. A human at the simulator running through the
  6 zones × 3 games is the closure gate.
- **Multiple feedback rounds are normal for first-of-kind layout adaptations.**
  The Video Mode small-zone shape didn't exist anywhere in the codebase before
  this phase; converging took 7 rounds. The redesign in `6d6d0b7` is now the
  canonical pattern for Phase 13's win/loss banner.
- **Don't re-litigate D-XX locks after they're validated by sim.** Round 7's
  user feedback ("perfect perfect perfect on all") locked the design. CONTEXT
  D-07/D-08/D-09 rewritten to match, with the original interpretation kept
  as a historical note so future agents don't revert.
